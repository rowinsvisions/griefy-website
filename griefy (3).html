Why Griefy exists

"Grief is one of the most universal human experiences. Yet also the loneliest."

After experiencing loss myself, I started building something. Griefy is built for anyone who has ever felt alone in their grief — a personal space that is always there when you need it, with no clinical language and no pressure to be okay.
